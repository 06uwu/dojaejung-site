PERFUME

NCT's latest subunit, DOJAEJUNG — comprised of members DOYOUNG, JUNGWOO, and JAEHYUN — have released their first mini album Perfume, a six-song collection of shimmering, romantic pop that showcases the members' smooth vocals.

Mini-Film Series

For Perfume, the trio created a mini-film series as a promotion called 'Triangular Theory of Love.' Each member was given their own character, fully immersing you in the vibe of the album. Each member also created their own perfume for the promotion, which they personally curated. You can even discover the fragrance notes by watching the films.

#PASSION Jaehyun

In the Triangular Theory of Love, Jaehyun plays a culinary chef going through a recent breakup. While working in the kitchen, time passes, and he is constantly consumed by thoughts of his former lover. At one point, his phone lights up—a message... it's from her... This scene highlights the intense passion and longing he still feels for her, aligning with the 'Passion' theme in the triangular theory of love. The triangular theory of love, developed by psychologist Robert Sternberg, identifies three components of love: passion, intimacy, and commitment. In this context, 'Passion' represents the intense emotions and physical attraction Jaehyun experiences.

#COMMITMENT Doyoung

Doyoung is lost in thought, reminiscing about a visit to the beach with a loved one. The film portrays his reflections on this memory, emphasizing his longing and commitment to this person. This scene highlights the 'Commitment' component in the triangular theory of love, which represents the decision to maintain a long-term relationship and the responsibility that comes with it. The triangular theory of love, developed by psychologist Robert Sternberg, identifies three components of love: passion, intimacy, and commitment. In this context, 'Commitment' represents the determination and responsibility Doyoung feels in his relationship.

#INTIMACY Jungwoo

Jungwoo plays a high school student who shares a close friendship with his classmates, portrayed by Jaehyun and Doyoung. The film depicts Jungwoo's daily school life and his growing feelings for a special person, with whom he constantly exchanges messages. At one point, the communication stops, leaving Jungwoo feeling sad. His friends encourage him to reach out, and eventually, he reunites with his loved one, highlighting their deep emotional bond and closeness. This film illustrates the concept of 'Intimacy' in the triangular theory of love, developed by psychologist Robert Sternberg. Intimacy refers to feelings of closeness, connection, and attachment within a relationship. It represents the emotional depth and trust that partners share with each other.
